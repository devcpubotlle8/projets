The Mystory of Mamo

Lupin, the master thief/spy/Jack of all Trades, has been executed,
but he is still alive, and not even Lupin himself knows how that is possible. While trying to figure out, however, he and his gang are
thrust into a conspiracy involving clones, Lupin's un-trustworthy rival Fujiko, and a minature mad-madman's plot to take over the world.
